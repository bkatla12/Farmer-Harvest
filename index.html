How to Play

  • You control the farmer (animated sprite).
  • Use ← → ↑ ↓ to move and collect crops.
  • Avoid scarecrows and crows — they reduce your score!
  • Collect power-ups: Speed Boost (blue) and Scythe (red)
  • Complete 3 levels with increasing difficulty to win!
  • Different crops give different points: Wheat (1), Pumpkin (3), Golden Apple (5)
  • Press P to pause/resume.
